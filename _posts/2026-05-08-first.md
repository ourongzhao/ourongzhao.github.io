Recent weeks have seen technology stocks rally sharply, propelled by intensifying investor enthusiasm around artificial intelligence themes. Breakthroughs in AI applications and infrastructure have fueled a broad sector advance, drawing capital into high-growth names despite broader market caution.

Meanwhile, the Iran conflict remains unresolved, injecting persistent volatility into energy markets and global risk sentiment. Heightened tensions continue to test supply chains and commodity pricing, creating a bifurcated environment where defensive sectors hold firm.

Market pricing dynamics have accelerated markedly compared to prior cycles, with news flow triggering rapid repricings across asset classes. This heightened sensitivity underscores the dual forces of technological optimism and geopolitical overhang, demanding agile positioning from investors.
